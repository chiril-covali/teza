# Toate Combinările de Dimensiune K

Slug: backtracking_all_combinations_of_size_k
Categorie: Backtracking

## Descriere
This generates an array of unique sub"sets" (represented by ascendingly sorted subarrays) of size k out of n+1 numbers from 1 to n.
